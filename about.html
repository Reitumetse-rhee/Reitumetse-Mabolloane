History

The Morija Arts and Cultural Festival is one of the biggest cultural events in Lesotho. It celebrates Basotho traditions including music, storytelling, crafts and food.

Why Tourists Love It

  • Authentic Basotho culture
  • Traditional music and dance
  • Local food and crafts
  • Art exhibitions

Target Visitors

The festival attracts local visitors, international tourists, students, artists, and cultural researchers interested in Basotho heritage.
